GENERAL DISEASE RULES:

The bot triggers outbreaks randomly on its own with 
no pattern the users can predict.
Only one race can have an active outbreak at a time.
A new outbreak cannot start until the current one ends.
Constellation race is fully immune to all diseases.
Shadow Veil users are fully immune to all diseases.
Users cannot go below 0 XP from disease drain.
Cures are race specific and only work on your own disease.
A user can hold one cure at a time.
If a user changes race while infected their infection 
is cleared automatically and the drain stops.
