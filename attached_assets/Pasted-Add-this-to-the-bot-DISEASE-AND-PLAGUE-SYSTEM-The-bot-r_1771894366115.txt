DISEASE AND PLAGUE SYSTEM:

The bot randomly triggers a disease outbreak for a 
specific race at random intervals with no admin input. 
When an outbreak begins the bot announces it in the 
group. The disease lasts a random duration between 
1 day and 7 days. When it ends the bot announces it.

Users protected by a Shadow Veil from the Cursed Bone 
are completely immune to all diseases and cannot 
contract or spread them.

Constellation race cannot contract any disease or plague.

---

HOW INFECTION WORKS:

When a disease is active for a race any user of that 
race who uses !leaderboard or !profile automatically 
contracts the disease. Once infected the user loses 
5hp every 5 minutes automatically until cured or 
the outbreak ends.

Private message when infected:
You have been infected.
The sickness spreads through your veins.
You are losing 100 XP every 30 minutes.
Visit the shop to find a cure.

GENERAL DISEASE RULES:

The bot triggers outbreaks randomly on its own with 
no pattern the users can predict.
Only one race can have an active outbreak at a time.
A new outbreak cannot start until the current one ends.
Constellation race is fully immune to all diseases.
Shadow Veil users are fully immune to all diseases.
Cures are race specific and only work on your own disease.
A user can hold one cure at a time.
If a user changes race while infected their infection 
is cleared automatically and the drain stops.
